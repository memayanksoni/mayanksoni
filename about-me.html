About Me

My Story

My name is Mayank Soni. I'm a creative and passionate individual who dreams big and aspires to contribute bigger. Along the way, I passionately strive to answer the "why" behind every problem. passionate about building digital experiences that leave a positive impact on people, environment and the society.
‍
I was brought up in Jabalpur, India where I learnt early on the importance of curiosity, getting things done, and grit - special thanks to my parents for it! After High school got myself into Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur to live one of my dream where I built life-long friendships and learned the importance of continuous growth. I used the breaks to wear various hats during internships at in London Strategy& Consulting Group, B:live, and Delhivery Pvt. Ltd. that helped me evolve towards solving problems or strategizing and taking initiatives to achieve a defined outcomes. In all the roles, I had the opportunity to delve into diverse roles right from strategy planning and development at London Strategy& Consulting Group to ground-level implementation at B:live and Delhivery. And in lieu of these experiences I landed myself into Product Management.

During my stay at IIT Kharagpur as well, I had been exposed to a myriad of challenging roles and responsibilities. Being a General Secretary - Social and Cultural, instilled in me substantial management and leadership abilities, by virtue of various challenging roles. As part of the Core Organizing Team of Spring Fest (India’s second-largest socio-cultural fest), from conceptualizing creative events to attract quality participation and sponsors, managing hospitality and accommodation of over 3800 guests, allowed me to assume different roles and work effectively in a team while simultaneously enabling me to build relationships with people from different walks of life.

To me, product management is a problem solving activity. As a PM, I understand that the perfect product not only involves visual aesthetics and the right tech stack, but even more importantly, an interface that helps solve the user's problem easily. This requires a deep understanding of human behaviour and psychology - the outcome of which greatly impacts the overall experience of the product, regardless of the platform.
